SNAP! go my bones

The Rules

The players have two decks, each having 26 cards . The players must draw cards in turn. When the two top cards have the same value the Pagliacci (Player 1) must press A and the Therapist(Player 2) must press L, the first player to press their button wins the snap pile. The first player to run out of cards is the one to lose.

The story

Pagliacci, the world famous clown, is playing a game of Snap! against his therapist for his eternal emotional wellbeing
The first player is Pagliacci and the second player is the therapist
